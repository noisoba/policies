Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026
Nature Walls is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Nature Walls does not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to use the app.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores the following data only on your device:

  • Your subscription status (premium or free) — stored in device preferences
  • Wallpaper images temporarily cached for faster loading

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

Pexels API
Nature Walls uses the Pexels API to display high-quality nature photographs. When you load wallpapers, your device makes requests to Pexels servers. Pexels may log standard request metadata according to their own Privacy Policy.

Google Play Billing
If you subscribe to Nature Walls Premium, the purchase is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information. Google's privacy practices are described in the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Permissions

Nature Walls requests only the permissions necessary to function:

  • Internet — to load wallpaper images from Pexels
  • Set Wallpaper — to apply wallpapers to your home and lock screens

5. Children's Privacy

Nature Walls does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no user-generated content and no social features.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
